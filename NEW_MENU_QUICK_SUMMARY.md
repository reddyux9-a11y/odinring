## 🎯 **How It Works**

### **Click "Items":**
- Opens: Merchant Items Manager
- Shows: Your products/services
- Actions: Add, edit, delete items

### **Click "Links":**
- Opens: Link Manager  
- Shows: Your links
- Actions: Add, edit, delete links
