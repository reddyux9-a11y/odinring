OdinRing Application Usage Guide

Version 1.0 | Last updated: 2026-03-27

This guide explains how to use OdinRing end-to-end: onboarding, activation, subscription, and how the platform supports personal branding and business growth.

1) How to Use OdinRing

Step 1: Sign In or Create an Account

  • Open the app and choose Sign In or Sign Up.
  • Use email/password or Google Sign-In.

Step 2: Complete Profile Setup

  • Go to Profile Settings.
  • Add display name, bio, profile image/logo, and mobile number.
  • Configure your social and contact details for better discoverability.

Step 3: Add Recommendations, Media, and Items

  • Recommendations (Links): Add websites, portfolio, social profiles.
  • Media: Upload images/videos for trust and engagement.
  • Items: Showcase products/services with pricing and details.

Step 4: Customize Your Page

  • Choose style, colors, fonts, and button appearance in Customization.
  • Use live preview to verify mobile-first appearance.

2) How to Activate Your Profile

  • Ensure your important recommendations/items are marked active.
  • Copy your public profile URL from the dashboard live preview section.
  • Share the profile URL in social bios, email signature, QR code, and business cards.
Tip: Keep your top 3 recommendations at the top for better conversion.

3) How to Subscribe

  • Navigate to Subscription from dashboard settings.
  • Select a plan based on your use case and required features.
  • Complete checkout and confirm activation status in Subscription Management.

Subscription Notes

  • New users may receive a trial period before paid billing starts.
  • You can upgrade/downgrade based on your growth stage.
  • Cancellation preserves access until the current billing period ends.

4) How OdinRing Helps Personal Branding

  • Creates a single professional identity page across all platforms.
  • Increases trust with profile image, bio, and structured recommendations.
  • Improves discoverability by organizing your important links in one place.
  • Supports consistent visual identity through customization.

5) How OdinRing Helps Business

  • Promotes products/services through items and media in one mobile-first page.
  • Enables quick customer actions (call, email, WhatsApp, website).
  • Tracks engagement (views/clicks) to optimize marketing decisions.
  • Reduces friction in lead capture and improves conversion readiness.

6) Best Practices

  • Use clear titles and concise descriptions for every recommendation/item.
  • Update your profile weekly with recent media and top offers.
  • Keep brand colors and tone consistent with your external channels.
  • Review analytics monthly and reorder content based on performance.

7) Support Contact

For help with onboarding, billing, or technical issues, contact: support@odinring.com
