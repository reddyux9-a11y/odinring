# OdinRing Demo Video Script

## Title
How to Use OdinRing, Activate Profile, Subscribe, and Grow Your Brand

## Target Duration
4 to 6 minutes

## Audience
- New users
- Personal brand creators
- Small business owners

## Scene-by-Scene Storyboard

### Scene 1 - Intro (0:00-0:20)
**Visual:** OdinRing logo + dashboard preview  
**Voiceover:**  
"Welcome to OdinRing. In this quick demo, you will learn how to set up your profile, activate your page, subscribe to a plan, and use OdinRing to grow your personal brand or business."

### Scene 2 - Sign In / Sign Up (0:20-0:45)
**Visual:** Auth screen showing Sign In and Sign Up tabs  
**Voiceover:**  
"Start by signing in with your email and password, or create a new account. You can also use Google Sign-In for faster access."

### Scene 3 - Profile Setup (0:45-1:35)
**Visual:** Profile settings tab (name, bio, avatar, contact info)  
**Voiceover:**  
"Next, complete your profile. Add your display name, short bio, and profile photo or logo.  
Set your contact details in Profile Settings. This helps visitors trust your profile and contact you quickly."

### Scene 4 - Add Recommendations, Media, and Items (1:35-2:25)
**Visual:** Dashboard sections: Recommendations/Links, Media, Items  
**Voiceover:**  
"Now add your recommendations, social links, portfolio links, media content, and product or service items.  
Use clear titles and short descriptions. This makes your page easy to scan and improves click-through."

### Scene 5 - Customize and Preview (2:25-3:00)
**Visual:** Customization settings + live preview panel  
**Voiceover:**  
"Customize colors, fonts, and style so your page matches your brand identity.  
Use the live preview to check how your profile appears on mobile."

### Scene 6 - Activate and Share (3:00-3:35)
**Visual:** Public profile URL copy + open in new tab  
**Voiceover:**  
"To activate your presence, copy your public profile URL and share it in your social bios, business cards, and campaigns.  
Keep your top recommendations first to drive better engagement."

### Scene 7 - Subscription Flow (3:35-4:20)
**Visual:** Subscription management page + choose plan + checkout  
**Voiceover:**  
"Go to Subscription to view your plan status, trial, and billing details.  
Choose or upgrade your plan based on your growth needs.  
Once checkout is complete, your premium features are activated."

### Scene 8 - Value for Personal Branding and Business (4:20-5:20)
**Visual:** Before/after profile examples, analytics highlights  
**Voiceover:**  
"For personal branding, OdinRing gives you one professional identity page to share everywhere.  
For business, it helps showcase offers, collect interest faster, and improve conversion with a mobile-first experience.  
Use analytics to understand what people click and continuously optimize your profile."

### Scene 9 - Outro + Support (5:20-5:40)
**Visual:** Customer Support tab  
**Voiceover:**  
"That is OdinRing in a nutshell. If you need help, visit Customer Support in Subscription Management or contact support@odinring.com."

## Recording Checklist
- [ ] Use 1080p resolution
- [ ] Keep cursor movement slow and intentional
- [ ] Zoom in on key actions (copy URL, choose plan, update profile)
- [ ] Add subtitles for key steps
- [ ] Include final CTA with support contact
